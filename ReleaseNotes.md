## MediaLens v1.1.31

### Summary

This release makes Local AI setup and day-to-day use much easier. MediaLens now offers a simpler recommended Gemma 4 setup, easier switching between downloaded model variants, and much more reliable local image descriptions.

### Highlights

- Gemma 4 now supports multiple downloadable model variants, so you can switch between installed versions without reinstalling everything.
- The Local AI Models window now guides most users through a simpler recommended setup while keeping advanced controls available.
- Gemma 4 description generation is more dependable, with cleaner final output and better support for GGUF and BF16 variants.

### Notes

- GPU detection, installer checks, and Local AI status messaging were improved to make troubleshooting and first-time setup less confusing.
